Donate Material


Thank you for considering making a material donation to Hope Foundation.
We welcome material donations such as household goods, clothing and personal items which will go towards helping families and people in need from various communities in Cameroon.

Hope Foundation welcomes the following material donations:


  • Household equipment:   mattresses, carpets, pots, books, toys
  • Clothing:                        Clean men´s, women´s and children´s clothing, bedding, shoes, curtains
  • Working tools:                sewing machines, mechanical or carpentry tools, farming and cookery tools
  • Transportation:              bikes, cars, trucks, tractors
  • Health items:                  medication, first aid material, hospital equipment, mosquito nets
  • Technology:                    Flat screen monitors, Laptops, CPUs, projectors, digital video cameras, copy machines, TVs


Donating material to Hope Foundation enables to benefit from the following advantages:


Save time and space!


By donating, you direclty saves money for rent of warehouse space, gains you more storage space by giving the items to a charity.


specifically for enterprises:

Space / Money:              You save expensive storage space rents and eliminate unused stock.

Marketing:            Donating material is also a good way to introduce your products to a new potential market group in an area of planned business                                      expansion.

Save the environment:  With a donation you are recyclable and do these things well preserved again accessible to the community. Customers find your business                                       attractive as you follow environmentally friendly principles.


If you have anything to donate, whether new or old, please contact us.
