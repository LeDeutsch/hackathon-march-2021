Teach German in Cameroon


We are looking for motivated young people, who want to teach German abroad and gain experience in teaching children or adults.

 

We’re glad that the first part of our “Rising International Bilingual School - RIBS” finally opened its doors to children and adults of the city of Bertoua in Cameroon! The school and Rising Training Center, Bertoua will provide to those in need direct support by offering high quality, low cost valuable education- training and proper sanitation. We are currently looking for volunteers interested in: 

·       Teaching German to children and adolescents (RIBS), as well as training adults (Rising Training Center, Bertoua). 


As a part of our project, we want to teach German at our school. Nevertheless, due to the limited amount of German teachers in the region we are looking for volunteers who could fill the respective positions. If you decide to join our program you will not be restricted to teaching German, you could also assist by teaching other subjects, such as sports, art or mathematics.


We are giving the volunteers the freedom to structure their own lessons as they please. Hence, they can teach the children with their own methods. However, the students only have a very basic knowledge of German.

Do you have experience in teaching languages or training? Do you want to use your skills to improve education standards? Are you interested in gaining great cultural insights and awareness as an international source by working among children as a teacher or adults as a trainer? If you’re ready to say yes to a youngster's future, apply here!


The school year begins at the 5th of October until the 31st of July. We do not expect the teachers to stay for the whole duration; however we are always looking for replacements to accommodate for the whole year.

Volunteers can also choose to take part of our holiday school program, which takes place during the summer holidays.





We are waiting for you to be part of our family!
