Holiday School and Cultural Exchange Program


With the opening of the first part of our "Rising International Bilingual School - RIBS", a place has emerged that offers the people of Bertoua high quality school education and workshops.


In the summer months, the school becomes a Holiday School!


Children from all circumstances and schools are welcome in this program.

Pupils who could not achieve a good performance in the previous school year or who want to gain new insights and knowledge of other subjects visit the Holiday School. With lots of fun, other children and various interactive methods, they learn new skills.

Do you have experience in teaching (e.g. foreign languages, natural sciences, history, art, computer science) or in carrying out training (e.g. everyday and health issues)?
Would you like to use your skills to embellish the holiday in a playful but educational way for children? Are you interested in gaining great intercultural insights and experiences by becoming part of the Holiday School and teaching children and adults?

If you are ready to make children laugh during the holidays, apply here or send us an email (office [at] hope-found.org)!



Information about duration, accommodation and cost you will find here!
